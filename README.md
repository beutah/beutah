## 👋 Hi, I'm Beutah

I spent over a decade in financial markets — running **fixed income and money market trading desks** at NIC Bank (now NCBA), UBA Kenya, and Equity Bank in Nairobi, Kenya — then founded and profitably exited a multi-venture company before moving to the US, where I worked in **risk management at Mascoma Bank**.

Today I run **[Bahari Advisory](https://bahariadvisory.com)**, a cloud and identity consulting practice for community banks, credit unions, and regulated financial institutions in the Pacific Northwest — building the technology those institutions actually need.

- 🔭 Currently building **DeskIQ** — an AI-powered, compliance-aware member support platform on Amazon Bedrock (RAG, guardrails, private VPC deployment)
- 🔐 Deep in **identity & access management**: Okta, Entra ID, Authentik, and identity lifecycle automation
- 📜 Certification path: AWS Cloud Practitioner ✅ → Okta Professional → AWS Solutions Architect Associate
- 🌍 Long-term arc: privacy law, AI governance, and US–East Africa advisory
